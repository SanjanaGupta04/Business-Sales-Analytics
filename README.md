# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes business sales data using Power BI to identify sales trends, profitable regions, top-performing products, and category performance.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Superstore Dataset

## KPIs

* Total Sales: 2.30M
* Total Profit: 286.40K
* Total Orders: 5K

## Dashboard Components

* Sales Trend Over Time
* Sales by Category
* Profit by Region
* Top 10 Products by Sales

## Key Insights

1. Technology generated the highest sales revenue.
2. West region produced the highest profit.
3. Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the top-selling product.
4. The business generated over 2.30M in total sales.
5. More than 5,000 orders were processed.

## Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on Technology products.
2. Expand successful strategies from the West region.
3. Maintain inventory for top-selling products.
4. Improve performance in lower-profit regions.
5. Use trend analysis for future sales planning.
